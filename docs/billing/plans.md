# Subscription Plans

Choose the plan that fits your needs.

## Available Plans

GroupListo offers several subscription tiers:

| Feature | Starter | Pro | Team | VIP |
|---------|---------|-----|------|-----|
| **Price** | Free | $X/mo | $X/mo | One-time |
| **Contacts** | Limited | More | Extended | Unlimited |
| **Team Members** | ❌ | Limited | Extended | Unlimited |
| **Import** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Activities** | Basic | Full | Full | Full |

:::note
Actual pricing and limits may vary. Check the app for current plans.
:::

## Free Trial

Every new account includes a **30-day free trial** with full access to all features.

### During Your Trial

- Access all Pro/Team features
- No credit card required
- Full import capabilities
- Team management access

### After Trial Ends

When your trial ends, you can:
1. **Subscribe** to a paid plan to continue with full features
2. **Use Starter** plan with limited features for free

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Your Plan

1. Click the menu (☰) in the top right
2. Select **Subscription**
3. View your current plan and usage

### Current Usage

The subscription page shows:
- **Contacts**: X of Y used
- **Team Members**: X of Y used
- **Plan Status**: Active, Trial, or Expired

### Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade:
1. Go to **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade** or **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Complete the payment

### Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel:
1. Go to **Subscription**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Follow the cancellation process

:::warning
When you cancel, you'll lose access to premium features at the end of your billing period.
:::
