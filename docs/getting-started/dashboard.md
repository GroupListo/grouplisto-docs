# Dashboard

Your home base in GroupListo.

## Overview

The Dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It provides a quick overview of your account and easy access to all main features.

## What's on the Dashboard

### Quick Stats

The dashboard displays key numbers at a glance:

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **My Contacts** | Total number of contacts in your list |
| **My Team** | Number of registered team members you've sponsored |

## Navigation

### Sidebar Menu

The main navigation is on the left side:

- **Dashboard** - Return to the overview
- **My Contacts** - Manage your contact list
- **My Team** - View and manage team members
- **Activities** - See all your outreach and meetings
- **Import Contacts** - Import from VCF, CSV, or Google

## Theming

GroupListo supports three theme modes:

1. **Light Mode** - Bright, white background
2. **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes, dark background
3. **System** - Follows your device's preference

To change the theme:
1. Click the menu button (â˜°) in the top right
2. In the Settings section, click your preferred theme icon
