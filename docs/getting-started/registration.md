# Registration

Learn how to create your GroupListo account.

## Creating an Account

There are three ways to register for GroupListo:

### Option 1: Email Registration

1. Go to [app.grouplisto.com](https://app.grouplisto.com)
2. Click **Register** or **Sign Up**
3. Fill in your details:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Password** (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click **Create Account**

### Option 2: Google Sign-In

1. Go to [app.grouplisto.com](https://app.grouplisto.com)
2. Click **Continue with Google**
3. Select your Google account
4. Grant the required permissions
5. You're in!

### Option 3: Facebook Sign-In

1. Go to [app.grouplisto.com](https://app.grouplisto.com)
2. Click **Continue with Facebook**
3. Log in to your Facebook account
4. Grant the required permissions
5. You're in!

## Using a Referral Code

If someone invited you to GroupListo, you can use their referral code during registration:

1. During the registration process, look for the **Referral Code** field
2. Enter the code provided by your sponsor
3. Complete the registration

:::tip Benefits of Using a Referral Code
When you register with a referral code:
- You'll automatically be added to your sponsor's team
- Your sponsor can see your progress and help you succeed
- You become part of a network marketing structure
:::

## Trial Period

Every new account comes with a **30-day free trial** that includes:

- Full access to all features
- Unlimited contacts during trial
- Access to import features
- Team management capabilities

After the trial ends, you can choose a subscription plan that fits your needs.

## Completing Your Profile

After registration, you may be prompted to complete your profile:

1. Add your **phone number**
2. Upload a **profile photo**
3. Set your **language preference**

This information helps your sponsor contact you and makes it easier for your team to recognize you.
