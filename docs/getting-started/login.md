# Logging In

Access your GroupListo account.

## Login Methods

### Email and Password

1. Go to [app.grouplisto.com](https://app.grouplisto.com)
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **Sign In**

### Social Login

You can also log in using:

- **Google** - Click "Continue with Google"
- **Facebook** - Click "Continue with Facebook"

:::note
To use social login, you must have previously registered with that provider.
:::

## Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

1. On the login page, click **Forgot Password?**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for the password reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

:::tip
If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
:::

## Session Management

- Your session stays active as you use the app
- For security, sessions may expire after a period of inactivity
- You can manually log out using the menu in the top right corner

## Staying Logged In

GroupListo remembers your login on your device. You won't need to log in every time you visit - unless:

- You log out manually
- Your session expires
- You clear your browser data
