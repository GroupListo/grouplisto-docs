# Contacts Overview

Learn how to manage your contact list in GroupListo.

## Your Contact List

The **My Contacts** section is where you manage all your contacts. This is the core of GroupListo - your personal database of people to follow up with.

## Contact List Features

### Table View

Your contacts are displayed in a table with:

- **Photo** - Avatar or profile picture
- **Name** - First and last name
- **Phone** - Primary phone number
- **Email** - Primary email address
- **Circle** - Category (Family, Friends, Work, etc.)
- **Priority** - Importance ranking
- **Status** - Current stage in your process

### Sorting and Searching

- **Search** - Type in the search box to find contacts by name, phone, or email
- **Sort** - Click column headers to sort ascending/descending
- **Filter** - Use filters to show only specific categories or statuses

### Pagination

Choose how many contacts to display per page:
- 25, 50, 100, 200, 300, 500, or All

## Quick Actions

For each contact, you can:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Edit** | Modify contact details |
| **Add Outreach** | Log a contact attempt |
| **Schedule Meeting** | Plan a meeting |
| **Delete** | Remove the contact |

## Understanding Contact Status

Contacts can be in different states:

- **Active** - Regular contact in your list
- **Activated** - Contact has registered as a team member
- **Hidden** - Contact marked as hidden from sponsor view
