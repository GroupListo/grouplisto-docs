# Tracking Outreach

Log your contact attempts to track your follow-up activity.

## What is an Outreach?

An **Outreach** (or Contact Attempt) is any time you try to reach out to a contact:
- Phone call
- Text message
- Email
- Social media message
- In-person conversation

## Recording an Outreach

### From the Contact List

1. Find the contact in your list
2. Click the **Actions** menu (â‹®)
3. Select **Add Outreach**
4. Fill in the details
5. Click **Save**

### From the Contact Detail Page

1. Open the contact
2. Go to the **Outreach** tab
3. Click **New Outreach**
4. Fill in the details
5. Click **Save**

## Outreach Fields

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Method** | How you contacted them | Yes |
| **Date & Time** | When you made the attempt | Yes |
| **Status** | Result of the attempt | Yes |
| **Reaction** | Their response | No |
| **Notes** | Additional details | No |

### Method Options

| Method | Use When |
|--------|----------|
| **Phone Call** | You called them |
| **SMS/Text** | Sent a text message |
| **Email** | Sent an email |
| **Social Media** | Messaged on Facebook, Instagram, etc. |
| **In Person** | Spoke face-to-face |

### Status Options

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Successful** | You reached them and had a conversation |
| **No Answer** | They didn't pick up or respond |
| **Busy** | They were busy, call back later |
| **No Interest** | They declined or showed no interest |
| **Scheduled** | You scheduled a follow-up |

## Viewing Outreach History

All outreach attempts are logged and visible:
- On the contact's detail page
- In the Activities feed
- In the Planner view

## Tips for Effective Outreach Tracking

:::tip Best Practices
1. **Log immediately** - Record attempts right after you make them
2. **Be specific** - Add notes about what was discussed
3. **Set follow-ups** - When scheduling, add the next attempt date
4. **Track all methods** - Every contact attempt matters
:::
