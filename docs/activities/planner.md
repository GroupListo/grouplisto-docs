# Using the Planner

View your activities in a calendar-style layout.

## Accessing the Planner

1. Go to **Activities** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Planner** tab

## Planner Features

### Calendar View

The Planner shows your scheduled activities in a timeline format:
- **Meetings** - Displayed with date, time, and contact name
- **Scheduled follow-ups** - Future contact attempts

### Date Range Filter

Filter the planner by date range:
1. Click on the **From** date picker
2. Select your start date
3. Click on the **To** date picker
4. Select your end date
5. The view updates automatically

### Quick Date Ranges

Common date ranges you might use:
- **Today** - All activities for today
- **This Week** - Current week's activities
- **This Month** - Full month view
