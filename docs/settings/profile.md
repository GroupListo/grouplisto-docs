# Profile Settings

## Accessing Settings

1. Click the menu (â˜°) in the top right corner
2. Click on your profile picture or name

## Profile Information

### Personal Details

You can update:
- **First Name**
- **Last Name**
- **Profile Photo** - Upload a picture that will appear in the app
- **Phone Number**
- **Email Address**

### Changing Your Email

1. Go to your profile
2. Update the email field
3. Save changes
4. Verify the new email if required

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to your profile
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password
4. Confirm the new password
5. Save changes

:::tip Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- We recommend using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
:::
